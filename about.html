Love and Love for Good Food

On April 19, 1912 a legend was born in Bacon Level, Alabama (a speck of land on the Georgia border). This legend, Marjorie Jane Durham, later became the great-grandmother of Marcia, Julia and Danielle Orozco.

Sitting at their great-grandmother's knee, these three young girls learned the secrets to a happy family, spending time together and enjoying good food. And the most important ingredient in any recipe is LOVE.

Marcia, Julia and Danielle now pass along to their Bronx neighborsthe love and love for food and family that was instilled in them by Grandma Marge. MJD Soul Food & Catering is the family restaurant that feels like you're sitting around Grandma Marge's kitchen table.
